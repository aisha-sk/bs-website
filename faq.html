Any City of Edmonton employee or department can request services. The team works closely with internal stakeholders to provide tailored IT solutions.

  • For existing applications – Submit an Application Service Request.
  • For new applications or business needs – Submit a Business Engagement Request.
  • Standard service requests: Typically 2-5 business days.
  • New application requests: Requires assessment and prioritization; timelines may vary.
  • Urgent requests: May be expedited based on business impact and resource availability.

Most services provided by Business Solutions are covered under department budgets, but certain projects (such as large-scale application development) may require additional funding. Costs, if any, will be discussed during project planning.

  1. Submit a service request through the designated platform.
  2. Clearly describe the issue or project requirements.
  3. Include relevant details such as deadlines, scope, and budget considerations.
